CTY FOUNDATION

Child hawking is a menace to the society, as it does more harm than good to our leaders of tomorrow, these kids carry heavy weights on their heads and it is our vision in Catch Them Young Foundation to relieve them of these weights.

Watch Introduction

OUR VISION

To empty the street of child hawkers



OUR MISSION

  • Educate and sensitize parents and guardians on the adverse effects of child hawking
  • Empower parents and guardians with funds that can enable them be comfortable
  • Equip parents and guardians with necessary skills that can enable them have careers of their own
  • Empower children with skills that can make them relevant to the world
  • Give appropriate help to kids that have been emotionally abused while hawking
  • Employing the help of security agents to reprimand parents and guardians who are found to be maltreating their wardens and children
  • Get the government to enact laws against child hawking and to maintain these laws
